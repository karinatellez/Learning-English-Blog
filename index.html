Thinking and media literacy

Today, we receive a lot of information every minute. Media literacy is essential to understand what is true and what is fake. Critical thinking helps us analyze the news, check the sources, and avoid misinformation. In my opinion, we should always question what we see on social media before sharing it. Being a critical thinker makes us better digital citizens.

Crime and punishment

Crime and punishment is a complex topic. There are many types of crimes in our society, and it is necessary to have a fair legal system. I believe that punishment should not only be about jail; it should also focus on rehabilitation and education. If we want a safer society, we must invest in better opportunities for everyone. Justice is not only about control, it is about giving people a second chance.
